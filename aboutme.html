Diego Bayani Ramil is an extraordinary pianist who began to play at 3 years old. Since then he has performed solo piano in numerous music festivals and events throughout the Bay Area including the SF Russian Center, SF Yerba Buena Arts Cen- ter and various other theaters. Last December he was invited to be a soloist for the Bach Piano Concerto in Dm with the El Camino Youth Symphony Orchestra at the Heritage Theatre in Campbell California. He is currently a 3rd year student at the San Francisco Conservatory of Music in a Pre-College Program. Additionally, Diego is the main pianist for the SF Latin Jazz Youth Ensemble and the pianist for Cuarteto Ramilonga, an Argentine Tango Quartet. Last Spring they were invited to perform in the Philippinies at a the 9th Annual Tango Festival held at the Culture Center of the Philippines in Manila. Diego has also participated in athletic team sports playing in a local soccer team and as part of the Rossi swim team. His main focus however, has been practicing Martial Arts since the age of 5. He competed in Judo for two years and is currently a junior black belt in Taekwondo. Prerequisites to becoming a black belt include volunteering to teach the curriculum to the students of lower belt levels and leading segments of the classes.
